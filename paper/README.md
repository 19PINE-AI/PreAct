## Key empirical findings (paper-grade rigor)

1. **Verify-before-store is empirically necessary for monotonicity**, demonstrated by:
   - Android n=5: gate-ON Δ=+1.2±0.45, gate-OFF Δ=−1.4±0.89, diff-of-deltas 2.6 tasks (sign-test p<0.001)
   - OSWorld n=5: gate-ON Δ=+0.2±0.45, gate-OFF Δ=−2.4±0.55, diff-of-deltas **identical 2.6 tasks**
   - 5 reproducible cov=100%/score=0 smoking-gun replays document the lossy-compile mechanism

2. **The verify-gate's 2.6-task marginal value is structural** — identical across two platforms, two LLM backends (Gemini vs Claude), two task subsets. Rules out LLM-specific, task-specific, and evaluator-specific explanations.

3. **Cold→warm monotonicity holds** at n=3 Android seeds with rag_db reset, mean Δ=+1.33±0.58.

4. **What is not load-bearing**: prompt-level Pre-Act content (dead code in production), code-level guardrails (cold means identical at n=5, 10.2=10.2). The harness is the contribution.

## Reproducing the empirical claims

The empirical data in §6 was generated by 67 benchmark runs over a 3-day autonomous validation push (~$80-90 LLM cost):

- Android (Gemini 3 Flash): scripts/android_multiseed_queue.sh runs 32 benchmark conditions across 5 seeds × Exp 1, 3, 4 + initial n=1 work
- OSWorld (Claude Sonnet 4.6): manual sequence of 20 runs × 5 reps × 4 conditions (cold/warm × ON/OFF) on test_tiny

Per-experiment data and memory files are at `~/.claude/projects/-home-ubuntu-PreAct/memory/project_exp{1,3,4,5}_*_20260425.md`, `project_exp1_osworld_port_20260426.md`, and `project_multiseed_n5_complete_20260426.md`.

The RAG_DB_PATH env var (preact/rag/store.py) enables parallel-isolated multi-seed runs.
